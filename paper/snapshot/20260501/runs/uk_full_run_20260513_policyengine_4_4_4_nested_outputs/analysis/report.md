## Population household-impact weights (headline)

Canonical country reports use full-population output-group weights: US weights come from the full Enhanced CPS, UK weights from the full enhanced FRS. Each output group's weight is the household-weighted mean of `|ref_ij| / max(|household_net_income_i|, sum_k |ref_ik|)` in the full weighting population, renormalized so weights sum to 1.
